Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to make it easier for customers to gain information about the company, help broaden the company's engagement with possible customers, and promoting the services offered by our company.

Audience

Who are the target customers: Our target customers are adventurous, outdoorsy individuals, aged 20-50, typically middle to upper class, often with families, who are interested in water rafting.

What do they want: These people could be seeking for additional excitement in their life, intend on making memories with family, or they wish to experience water rafting.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website: What can be expected when viewing this website is information about the various activities and services provided.
